Dagoth Ur, originally Voryn Dagoth,[1] also known as the Sharmat,[2][3] as well as Awakened Lord of the Sixth House,[4] Lord Dagoth and Father of the Mountain to his followers,[5] was the immortal Lord High Councilor of House Dagoth. In Tribunal mythology, he is demonized as the Devil and the Enemy, False Dreamer,[3] Devil Dagoth Ur,[5] and Devil of Dagoth.
